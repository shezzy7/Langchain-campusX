RAG is technique that combines information retrieval with language generation,where amodel retrieves relevant documents from a knowledge base and the uses them as a context to generate accurate and grounded responses.
Benefits of using RAG:-
    i-Use of up-to-data information
    ii-Better privacy
    iii-No limit of document size
We can make our models to reponse us from a given set of data.And we can implement this functionality with the help of RAG.

RAG is consist of 4 major components :-
    i-Document Loader
    ii-Text Splitter
    iii-Vector Database
    iv-Retrievers
